Interactive Flashcards

The Active Recall Card

Reading a page over and over is passive study. We show you a question, let you try to recall the answer, and then let you check the explanation. It keeps your brain awake and active!

  • Covers direct line-by-line questions from NCERT
  • Rates cards based on how well you recalled them (SM-2 algorithm)
  • Minimalist, dark-mode design that is gentle on your eyes
Analytics & Streaks

Supporting Your Consistency

It's easy to lose motivation during prep. MEDNEET helps you keep track of your daily study streaks, awards XP badges for review, and highlights your weak spots so you can revise them before your coaching tests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers to clear up any doubts about the application.

How does the spaced repetition sync work? +
All your flashcard data, strengths, and study history are synced automatically to secure databases (Firebase Firestore). You can switch between devices easily, and your progress will be waiting for you.
Is the app completely free to use? +
We offer core study modules and general flashcards for free so everyone can study. Some premium features and high-yield chapters require an upgrade to help cover our server and AI generation costs.
How can I delete my account and data? +
We make data safety simple! You can delete your account instantly from the Profile settings inside the app. If you need any help, just email us at notesmedicose@gmail.com and we will wipe your records manually.
