Got Questions? (We Know You Do)

Common doubts answered by our developers (who were once as stressed as you).

Is this app offline or online? +
It is a hybrid web/native app! It is wrapped in Capacitor for Android and loads the Next.js production server, meaning you get real-time database sync in Firestore and live AI-powered questions.
What algorithm is used for the flashcards? +
We use a modified version of the SM-2 Spaced Repetition Algorithm. It schedules cards based on your difficulty rating, pushing easy cards out by days and bringing difficult concepts back in minutes.
Can I request account deletion? +
Yes, Google Play Store has strict policies on this, and we respect your data! You can delete your account instantly from the Profile settings inside the app, or email us at support@medneet.com for manual deletion.
