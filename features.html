Take a Tour of MEDNEET

NEET prep is tough, so we made our application interface direct and simple. Here is a walkthrough of every screen and feature with real screenshots.

SCREEN 01

The Study Dashboard

Your main cockpit when you open the app. We designed it to keep you focused on your daily targets without cluttering your mind.

  • Daily Streaks: Keeps track of how many consecutive days you reviewed. Maintaining a streak is a great way to stay regular!
  • Daily Goal Progress: A visual tracker showing how close you are to your daily target reviews (e.g. 20 cards).
  • Subject Selectors: Quick entry points into Biology, Chemistry, or Physics modules.
SCREEN 02

Flashcards Lobby

The lobby lets you choose which subject you want to review. It also displays helpful card metrics.

  • Chapter Filters: Select a specific chapter (like Animal Kingdom or Electrostatics) or review the entire subject at once.
  • Memory Stats: Shows the total number of cards available, how many are new, and how many are due under spacing guidelines.
  • Study Modes: Choose "Review Due" to revise cards that are ready, or choose "New Cards" to add fresh concepts.
SCREEN 03

Study Core: Question Card

This is where active recall happens. Instead of passively reading textbooks, you are prompted with direct NCERT line-by-line questions.

  • Clear Questions: Sentences built directly from your textbook, focusing on high-yield exam topics.
  • No Clutter: Minimalist glassmorphic layout to keep your eyes comfortable and distractions low.
  • Self-testing: Read the question, try to recall the answer in your head, then press the Spacebar or click the card to flip it.
SCREEN 04

Study Core: Answer & Explanation

Once flipped, the card reveals the correct answer and a complete, student-friendly explanation.

  • NCERT Reference Notes: Shows the exact reference details and formula equations (formatted beautifully in LaTeX).
  • Self-Rating Buttons: Rate your recall strength (Again, Hard, Good, Easy). This feeds into the SM-2 algorithm to schedule the card's next review time.
  • Weakness Logging: Any card rated "Again" is automatically added to your home screen weakness checklist so you don't ignore it.
SCREEN 05

MCQ Practice Arena

Need to build exam-level speed? The Practice Arena serves timed, multiple-choice questions matching direct NEET patterns.

  • Timed Sessions: Helps you train your focus under high-pressure exam speeds.
  • Accuracy Meter: Shows real-time correct-to-wrong ratios to track your performance.
  • Syllabus Mapping: Questions are balanced across the core chapters of Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.
SCREEN 06

Equation & Formula Sheet

A centralized directory of every important physics and chemistry equation. If you need to quickly revise before a test, this is your best friend.

  • Categorized Lists: Equations sorted neatly by subject, chapter, and topic.
  • Speed drills: Quickly scroll and test if you remember the variables and units for each equation.
  • Clean Math Layout: Rendered with high-quality math styling (KaTeX) for clear readability.
SCREEN 07

Community Leaderboard

Healthy competition is a great motivator. The Leaderboard shows where you stand in the MEDNEET aspirant community.

  • XP Scores: Earn experience points (XP) for reviewing flashcards and getting MCQs right.
  • Community Rankings: See rankings of other aspirants in real-time. It keeps you inspired to study a bit more.
  • Secure profile alias: Displays only your chosen username and streak emoji to protect your personal identity.
SCREEN 08

Your Personal Profile

Track your overall consistency over the week and manage your account settings securely.

  • Focus Calendar: Visually logs your daily study history and streak milestones.
  • AI Settings: Adjust generation preferences or configure keys for custom AI tutor explanations.
  • Simple Account Actions: Update your profile display name, verify your email, or securely request account deletion under Account Settings.
