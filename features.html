🔧 HOW IT WORKS

Under the Hood of MEDNEET

We didn't just dump 27,000 questions in a spreadsheet. We designed highly optimized modules to address the biggest pain points of cracking NEET UG.

🩺 MODULE 01

SAGE Biology: NCERT Line-by-Line

NEET Biology is 100% NCERT. If you miss a single line in a diagram or a summary, say goodbye to a perfect 360/360. SAGE Biology breaks down every single paragraph in NCERT into active-recall questions.

  • Line-by-line verification: Questions are extracted directly from NCERT textbook sentences so you know the book by heart.
  • Image Hotspots: Visual flashcards targeting labelings in complicated diagrams like the Nitrogen Cycle and Human Heart.
  • Coaching Killers: Specifically designed to help you crush assertion-reasoning questions that trip up even the top rankers.

10,000+ Flashcards

🔥 MODULE 02

Terracotta Chemistry: Mechanisms & Formulas

Chemistry is a mess of contradictions (looking at you, inorganic exceptions). Terracotta Chemistry organizes organic naming reactions, inorganic electronic trends, and physical chemistry formulas into easily digestible recall sequences.

  • Organic Reaction Builder: Flashcards testing reactants, intermediates, and final products of key reactions.
  • Exception Busters: Custom cards addressing every weird exception in Inorganic Chemistry (Inert pair effect, electron gain enthalpy trends, etc.).
  • Physical Chemistry Checklist: Immediate numerical formula recalls (thermodynamics, electrochemistry, chemical kinetics).
⚡ MODULE 03

Sky Physics: Formulas at Speed

Physics in NEET is not about complex calculus; it's about speed and direct formula application. If you have to derive a formula in the exam hall, you have already run out of time. Sky Physics drills the formulas into your brain.

  • Formula Recall: Rapid-fire flashcards to check if you remember formulas for rotational mechanics, kinematics, and electromagnetism.
  • Direct Applications: Direct MCQ application questions testing whether you can substitute values under high time pressure.
  • Dimension & Unit Checkers: Fast checks on units and dimensions to eliminate option choices instantly.
